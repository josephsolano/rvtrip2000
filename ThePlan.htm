Skyway Drive-In

Rich:

If you find yourself on I-80 headed through Ohio, and you're passing through Akron (or even if not!), you may want to check out Jimmy Buffet's favorite restaurant, Skyway Drive-In.  Skyway has been in the same place

under family ownership since 1949, and has almost not changed their menu since.  There are some of the best burgers, fried mushrooms, onion rings, shakes that you can find!  Everything done exactly how you order it!  Guys

will run out to your car and give you excellent service.  I used to work there, so I'm very biased.

 

If you take the I-77 exit from I-80 and head South to Akron, you would want the Ghent Road exit.  Turn right off the ramp onto Ghent Road.  After almost a mile, you'll be forced to go soft left or hard right.  Turn left onto Market Street (Route 18).  Within a couple blocks, it will be on the left.S

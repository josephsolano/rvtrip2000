The inside of our Winnebago

The inside of our Winnebago is more spacious than you might expect.  It is a little difficult to tell from these pictures but it is a little smaller than most New York apartments, about 300 square feet.

 

Here is the floor plan of the interior:

 

 

The interior contains a living room, kitchen, dining room table, bathroom, and bedroom.  It has a:

 

  • 19in and 13in TV
  • VCR
  • DVD player
  • Global Positioning System
  • 3 lounge chairs including the driver’s and passenger chair
  • A full size couch that folds down into a bed
  • A dinette area that folds down into a bed
  • Stove and oven
  • Full size sink
  • Microwave oven
  • Shower and tub
  • Marine toilet and bathroom sink
  • Queen size bed
